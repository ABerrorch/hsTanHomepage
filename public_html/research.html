Research

My research interests lie in Networking Algorithm Design and System Implementation. Specifically, recently I am working on some fundamental problems in mobile edge computing and datacenter networks (e.g., flow routing and scheduling, job dispatching and scheduling, resource allocation). We are dedicated in designing novel algorithmic solutions based on online/distributed/learning algorithms to enhance the mobile computing systems.


Acknowledgement: My current research is partially supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Education, Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd., and USTC.
